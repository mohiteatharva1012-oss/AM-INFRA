Everything you need to know before placing your first order with AM Group & Co.

For cement, our minimum order is 50 bags (2.5 MT). For TMT steel, the minimum is 1 ton. For SRJ stirrups, minimum is 100 kg. For smaller quantities, please contact us and we will try to accommodate your requirement.

Yes. For every JSW Neosteel and SAIL TMT order, we can provide the official mill test certificate (MTC) from the factory. This is critical for government and RCC projects. Just mention it at the time of ordering and it will accompany your delivery.

For locations within 50 km of Dharashiv, we offer same-day delivery if ordered before 2:00 PM. For locations between 50–150 km, delivery is the next morning. Our own fleet of transit mixers and trucks ensures reliable, on-time delivery every time.

We accept NEFT / RTGS / IMPS bank transfer, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and cheque (for established accounts). For first-time orders, advance payment is required. Repeat clients with a track record may be offered credit terms — contact us to discuss.
